    About ICMA International

    Established in 1951, the Institute of Cost and Management Accountants of Pakistan is the driving force behind the profession of Management Accounting in Pakistan. Our mission is to regulate and promote this dynamic field, empowering professionals to reach new heights of success.

    With the authority vested in us by the Act and Regulations, we take pride in providing top-notch education, assessing competencies, and conferring internationally recognized post-graduate professional certifications. Our prestigious designations, including :

    Affiliate Financial Accountant
    Associate Cost and Management Accountant
    Chartered Management Accountant
    Fellow Cost and Management Accountant
